Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a fictional superhero created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko for Marvel Comics. First appearing in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962, he has become one of the most iconic and relatable superheroes of all time. Peter is an ordinary teenager who gains extraordinary powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He develops abilities such as wall-crawling, superhuman strength, agility, spider-sense, and the intelligence to invent his own web-shooters.
